# Trademark Guidelines

The Wayfindr name, logo, and related project marks are not licensed under the repository's software licenses.

The intent is simple:

- You may accurately refer to the Wayfindr project.
- You may fork the software under the applicable license terms.
- You may not imply that an unofficial fork, hosted service, package, or integration is official.
- You may not use Wayfindr branding in a misleading way.
- Unofficial distributions and hosted services should use distinct names and branding.

Detailed trademark rules should be written before public launch, before third-party hosting, or before accepting broad external contributions.
