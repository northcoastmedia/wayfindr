# Product Principles

Wayfindr exists to help support teams understand where users are stuck and guide them forward without unnecessary friction.

## Principles

- Keep the install surface small.
- Make support context visible without exposing sensitive data.
- Require consent for cobrowsing.
- Prefer simple, durable support records over bloated helpdesk workflows.
- Make self-hosting real, not decorative.
- Keep the hosted service useful because it is easier and better operated, not because Community Edition is intentionally hostile.
- Use AI as an assistive tool only when it improves a concrete support or development workflow.
- Document product decisions publicly when they affect users or contributors.
